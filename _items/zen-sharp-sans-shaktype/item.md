## Summary
Zen Sharp Sans is a precision-driven sans-serif with sharp terminals and clean geometry, showcased through a ~20-slide Instagram carousel with bold editorial compositions. The ninja/monk thematic framing ("sharp as a blade, calm as a monk") demonstrates the typeface's dual personality — aggressive precision with measured poise. Designed by Shaktype with guidance from Tina Smith Design.
